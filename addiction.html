Introduction to Addiction

Addiction is a complex condition, a brain disorder that is manifested by compulsive substance use despite harmful consequences. Over 20 million people in the U.S. are affected by addiction.

Types of Addiction

  • Substance Addiction: Alcohol, drugs (illicit and prescription)
  • Behavioral Addiction: Gambling, internet use, shopping

Causes of Addiction

The roots of addiction are multifaceted, including biological, psychological, and environmental factors.

Signs and Symptoms of Addiction

Common signs include withdrawal from social activities, noticeable changes in appearance, and persistent mood swings.

Treatment Options

Effective treatment may involve detoxification, therapy (like CBT), and support groups.

Resources for Help

If you or someone you know is struggling, consider reaching out to hotlines and local treatment centers for immediate support.
