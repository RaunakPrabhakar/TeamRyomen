Understanding Attrition

Attrition refers to the gradual reduction of a workforce due to employees leaving and not being replaced. Understanding the dynamics of attrition is crucial for organizational health.

Causes of High Attrition

  • Job dissatisfaction
  • Poor management
  • Inadequate compensation
  • Lack of career advancement opportunities

Impact of High Attrition

High attrition can lead to decreased productivity, low morale, and increased costs associated with hiring and training new employees.

Strategies to Reduce Attrition

Implementing effective employee engagement strategies, enhancing workplace culture, and providing growth opportunities can help retain employees.
